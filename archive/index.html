AI-powered workflow automation for specialty dental practices

FlowSurge turns referrals and operational workflows into a fully automated system — increasing case acceptance and production while cutting manual admin work for your team.

What FlowSurge does

  • Increases revenue — automatically surfaces and follows up on high-value referral and treatment opportunities.
  • Decreases manual work — automates outreach, reminders, status updates, and task routing.

About

Our mission

FlowSurge is a healthcare technology company building AI-powered tools that help specialty dental practices operate more efficiently. We automate the manual, error-prone workflows that slow down referral processing, patient communication, and day-to-day operations — so clinical teams can focus on patient care.

Founder

Amit Kulkarni — tech executive (ex-Google, Amazon, Microsoft) with deep experience building AI and workflow platforms at scale.

Services

What we automate

FlowSurge provides AI-driven tools purpose-built for specialty dental and oral surgery practices.

Referral Automation

  • AI-powered intelligent routing and prioritization of new referrals
  • Automated follow-ups with patients and referring offices
  • Visibility into missed consults, unscheduled treatment, and incomplete cases

Patient Communication

  • SMS appointment reminders and scheduling confirmations
  • Post-visit follow-up messages and care notifications
  • Referral coordination and specialist scheduling updates

Workflow Automation

  • Standard operating processes embedded as automated workflows
  • Task lists auto-generated for front desk, coordinators, and clinical teams
  • Audit-friendly logs showing that SOPs were followed on every case

SOP Codification

  • Convert tribal knowledge into repeatable, trackable processes
  • Every referral, case, and follow-up runs through a standardized workflow
  • Configurable rules that match how your practice actually operates
